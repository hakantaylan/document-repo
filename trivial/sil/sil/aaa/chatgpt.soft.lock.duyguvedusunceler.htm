Key concepts

  1. Flush (entityManager.flush() or repository.flush())

  • Forces Hibernate to send SQL statements (INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE) to the database immediately.

  1. Read-committed isolation (default in most DBs)

  • A transaction only sees committed changes from other transactions.

  • Your own transaction sees all of its changes, whether flushed or not.

  • Other transactions cannot see your uncommitted changes, even if you flushed.
